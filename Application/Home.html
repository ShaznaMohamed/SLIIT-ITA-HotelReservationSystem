The tropical climate, spectacular art, thriving culture, endless shopping, unbelievable dining and lively nightlife – this is the city of Colombo. Amid the whirl of excitement you will find The Sienna, a five star word- class Hotel. A cultural oasis amid a bustling current, The Sienna continues the tradition of defining luxury through unmatched service with a modern spirit.

While traditions remain, such as the superb ambience of the legendary ruins, kings' court, Avalanche, Colombo's premier destination for Afternoon Tea, exciting new offerings include the elegant Champagne Bar and stylish spa and gym, as well as the acclaimed international cuisine range and The Sienna restaurant and bars. The Sienna Hotel offers 200 distinctive guestrooms, including 89 suites, which boast the largest square footage of any luxury hotel in Sri Lanka. From the sumptuous décor to the impeccable white glove service, The Sienna is an extraordinary hotel that offers every indulgence. For our suite guests, The Sienna is pleased to offer Butler service 24 hours a day. Old world luxury and elegance are complemented by discreet placement of the latest technology thoughtfully integrated throughout each room bringing guests high-definition television and high-speed wi-fi.
