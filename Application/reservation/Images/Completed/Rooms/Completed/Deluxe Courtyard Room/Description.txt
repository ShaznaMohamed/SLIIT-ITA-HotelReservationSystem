The elegant Deluxe Courtyard Room is exceptionally spacious with a comfortable sitting area. 
The bathroom is finished with inlaid “earth stone” mosaics in floral motifs, with a bathtub as well as separate shower. 
Bathroom details include 24-carat gold plated Sherle Wagner faucets and accessories with hand crafted solid white marble 
vanities. These well-appointed rooms are located throughout the hotel on floors 5 to 10 facing the interior landscaped
