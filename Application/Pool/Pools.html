PARTIER

You have heard about all the fun that happens at pools in Hotel Sienna. As you fill your bag with stuff for Sri Lanka vacation you can only think of one thing. What will the pools Hotel Sienna be like? Hotel Sienna has two pools which contain all facilities for adults and kids.

Our pools are attended by lifeguard & Pool attendant, but please supervise your children closely. Swimwear is a must and taking Alcohol while in the pool is not allowed.

Hotel Sienna attracts a young crowds,drawn to the swimming pool as much for its floating craps table as its social scene. Coconut trees and and sculptured landscaping give the whole swimming pool area a Mediterranean feel.

You can use this pool to conduct their events and parties. If you are going to conduct the parties in this pool what all you have to do is to reserve the pool before conducting the party.

  • Open: 8am - 8pm daily

  • Depth: 2.5 ft - 6.2 ft

SPIRAL

Partial is one of architect Geoffrey Bawa's ingenious concepts.
The pool, right next to the main lobby, guides your eye to the ocean as you enter the hotel. It looks as if the edge of the pool blends with the water of the ocean, giving the impression that you are in the sea, although the beach separates the pool and the ocean.

The traditional boat placed in the middle of the pool is an icon of the hotel adds a touch of local culture. Poolside seating, bars and the Mustard Coffee Shop make this a favourite place to soak, sunbathe or enjoy a cocktail.

  • Open: 8am - 8pm daily

  • Depth: 2.5 ft - 5.11 ft
