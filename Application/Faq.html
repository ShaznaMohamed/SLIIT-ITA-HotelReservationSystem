How do I reserve a facility offered by The Sienna?

Click on the "Reservations" tab which you'd be able to find in our menu bar. This will redirect
you to a page where you could select the type of facility you'd like to reserve and allows you to do so
through a brief series of related questions needed in order to successfully reserve a facility

How can I get more details not displayed in the website ?

Click on the "Contact Us" page tab in the menu bar where you can leave brief messages along with your
email address. Our management will correspond to your message at their earliest. Or you could
directly call us on or hotline number displayed in the same page. Click on the number or link to
directly call us through a caller application on your PC or mobile device such as Skype, iCall, etc

Why is it necessary to provide my personal information while reserving ?

As our Hotel Management has become lineate and no direct dealing or interaction is done with the customer,
it is necessary to get assured that the Sienna is dealing with a serious Reservation. So please be kind enough to fill out
your details as required.

Who is responsible for event handling at The Sienna ?

Once a customer arrives at the Sienna a personal event handler can be booked in order to get your vacation sorted out.
Our event handlers are well versed in all the fields relevant to tourism and management. Hence forth, they'd be glad enough to
guide you on relevant and requested visit sites, travel and transport, food and beverages, entertainment, flight details, etc
